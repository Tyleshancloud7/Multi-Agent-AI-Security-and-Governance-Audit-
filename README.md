# Multi-Agent-AI-Security-and-Governance-Audit-
Organization Profile 
* Name: Cypronetics AI Labs
* Industry: Technology / AI Services
* AI Architecture: Five interacting AI agents (Planner, Researcher, Reporter, Scheduler, Retirement)
* Focus: AI decision-making, natural language generation, and model-to-model communication

📚 Frameworks & Standards
* NIST CSF 2.0 – Cybersecurity Framework
* NIST AI RMF 1.0 – AI Risk Management Framework
* ISO/IEC 23894:2023 – AI Risk Management
* MITRE ATLAS – Adversarial AI Threats
* OWASP Top 10 for LLMs – Common AI/LLM vulnerabilities
* EU AI Act & U.S. AI Executive Orders – Regulatory Compliance

🔍 Assessment Areas
1️⃣ AI Governance & Accountability
* Evaluate AI governance board, roles, and responsibilities
* Assess ethics, fairness, and transparency policies
* Confirm existence of AI risk registers
2️⃣ AI Asset & Supply Chain Inventory
* Track AI models, datasets, APIs, and embeddings
* Review third-party model dependencies
* Verify model provenance and SBOM (Software Bill of Materials)
3️⃣ AI Security Controls
* Identity & Access Management (least privilege, zero-trust)
* Data encryption and secure agent-to-agent communication
* Prompt injection and model inversion protections
4️⃣ Adversarial Detection & Monitoring
* AI-native monitoring for prompt injection and data poisoning
* Model drift and bias detection
* Real-time dashboards for AI system anomalies
5️⃣ Incident Response & Recovery
* AI-specific incident response playbooks
* Rollback strategies for compromised models
* Resilience testing (chaos engineering, red-team simulations)
